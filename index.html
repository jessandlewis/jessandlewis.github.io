How it began

Our Story

It all started in Edinburgh in 2017, when a group of mutual friends brought two people together who had absolutely no idea what was coming. The moment Lewis offered Jess his last slice of pizza, something clicked — and Jess, never one to let a good deed go unrewarded, sealed the deal shortly after with a home-cooked Spanish chicken recipe that Lewis still talks about to this day.


From that first night, they discovered a shared world of sarcastic humour, film and TV quotes traded at speed, good music, and dancing with friends until far too late. Life eventually took them south to London, but their story was always heading somewhere more scenic.


In September 2024, on a cliffside in Menorca — the early morning light spilling across a bright blue sea below — Lewis got down on one knee. Jess said yes.

Nestled in the East Neuk of Fife, The Cow Shed is a beautiful rustic steading with sea views across the Firth of Forth to the Bass Rock and East Lothian. Full of original character and charm, it's the perfect blank canvas for our day.


The venue sits just outside the picturesque coastal village of Crail — one of Scotland's most beautiful corners, with stunning beaches, traditional harbours, and rolling Fife farmland all around.

Getting here & staying over

Travel & Accommodation

The Cow Shed is located just outside Crail in the East Neuk of Fife — about 10 minutes' drive from St Andrews and roughly 1 hour from Edinburgh. Here's everything you need to know about getting there.

🚗

By Car

From St Andrews, take the A917 towards Crail. Just before entering the village, take the sharp right onto the B940 — follow the brown tourist signs. There is on-site car parking with pink gravel.

Get directions ↗

🚆

By Train

The nearest train station is Leuchars, with regular services from Edinburgh (approx. 1 hr) and Dundee (approx. 20 mins). From Leuchars, a taxi or pre-booked transfer to the venue takes around 25 minutes.

✈️

By Air

Edinburgh Airport is the most convenient, around 1 hour 20 minutes by car. Dundee Airport also operates limited services and is closer to the venue at around 45 minutes.

🏡

Accommodation

The Cow Shed has on-site cottages sleeping up to 46 guests in total — perfect for staying close to the celebrations! More details and booking information to follow.

Nearby towns including Crail, Anstruther, and St Andrews also offer a wide range of hotels, B&Bs, and holiday cottages.
